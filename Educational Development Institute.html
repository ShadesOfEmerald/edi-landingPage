Welcome To EDI

Educational Development Institute (EDI) is an educational organization providing professional training programs, consultancy services, recruitment services and a TESOL training program in Cambodia. Our long term objective is to enable to expand learning and enrichment opportunities for all people. We do this by supporting and sharing effective ideas and practices. EDI is committed to raising the performance of businesses by championing management and setting standards in management development.

FAQs

What training are available at EDI ?

At EDI, we provide you with three types of courses for your choices:

Public Training:

  • Business Management, Sales And Marketing
  • Management And Leadership
  • Training And Development
  • Human Resource Management
  • Accounting And Finance
  • Project Management And Research
  • TESOL Certifications 
     

Executive Certificate:

  • Human Resource Management
  • Entrepreneurship And SME Management
  • High-Impact Leadership 
  • School Management
  • Research And Development
  • Sales And Marketing
     

Customized Training Program:
All public and executive courses at EDI can be customized, designed and delivered exclusively for a client organization, allowing great flexibility and accessibility.

How do I know whether I am registered for a training course?

You will receive confirmation of your course registration by email. Your registration is only complete with this written confirmation and/or confirmation call from our EDI team. 

How do I register for a training ?

In Writing: Online, using the registration form or by email to training@edi-cambodia.org 

Online registration form is available on the right side of each course

What is the maximum number of participants for the training courses ?

The maximum number of participants for practical training sessions are 20 to 25 person. In order to ensure successful learning, working takes place in small groups (approx. 4 to 5 persons). For theoretical training courses, the maximum number of participants is limited to the number of seats in the training rooms.

Will training documents be provided?

Appropriate training documents will be provided for all training courses. For all other training courses, printed training documents will be provided as a hand-out book and EDI Noted Book.
